Courage the Cowardly Dog

Courage the Cowardly Dog was a show originally aired in 1996. It was by war one of the freakiest shows I have ever seen. It contained some weird, trippy images. The earliest episodes had the strangest storylines and characters, but still held up as one of, if not one of the greatest shows of the 90s era. Please, PLEASE give this one a watch. It'll definitely be worth it!
