Rugrats

Rugrats was a very popular 90s show that introduced a cast of babies and their loving parents in 1991. The loveable babies went on many adventures and learned many lessons along the way. Tommy, along with Chuckie, Angelica, and the rest of the gang always had a way of trying to be the best babies they could be. This show is not only visually appealing; it is heartwarming, fun, and a joy for the whole family :)
