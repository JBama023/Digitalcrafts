LEGENDS OF THE HIDDEN TEMPLE

Legends of the Hidden Temple was a Nickelodeon TV Game show that aired from 1993 to 1995. The show was composed of six teams (Red Jaguars, Blue Barracudas, Green Monkeys, Orange Iguanas, Purple Parrots, and Silver Snakes) that competed in physical and mental events. There were two kids per team, and all six teams would compete in three rounds until there was only one team left. The final round would be the hardest challenge of the show, the Temple Run. This was one of the best best game shows to watch as a kid and definitely good to watch for a nostalgia kick for any 90s kids!
